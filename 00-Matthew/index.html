Why upgrade an electrical panel?

Your home's ability to consume electricity safely can be significantly increased by switching to a modern electrical panel. With a 100-amp panel, it's likely that your home is operating close to capacity even if your breakers don't trip frequently.

Older electrical panels demand more energy to function than a modern, fully-updated panel. Utilizing less energy will ultimately result in cheaper utility costs and more money in your pocket.

Cost efficient

Because a newer panel will be designed to properly channel power to where it needs to be inside your home, you can typically save between 10 and 30% on your monthly electrical use.

Increases your house value
